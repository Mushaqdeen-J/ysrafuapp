Campus Experience Hostel Facilities

Dr. YSR Architecture and Fine Arts University provides residential facilities separately for boys and girls. Most of our students are moving away from home for the first time, so we wanted to provide them comfortable, safe and homely set-up in the campus. All the rooms have been designed to provide adequate moving space and ample air and light.


The hostel facility includes a bed and a study table per student. This facility will be on a sharing basis with the fellow students. In order to facilitate comfortable stay for our students, we have decided the sharing capacity to max 3 students for smaller rooms and 4 persons for large rooms. Dining facility will be in the Hostel premises.


Hostel facility will be maintained Hygienically, students will be served veg/non- veg food on a weekly basis. Hostel facility will be allotted on first cum first serve basis. Hostel Charges will be Rs. 4000/-(approximately) per month inclusive of everything.


HOSTEL FACILITY IS AVAILABLE FOR BOYS AND GIRLS(Academic year 2020-2021)
