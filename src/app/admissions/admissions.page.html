The admission procedure will be through the following written examinations for the following courses.

Bachelor of Architecture - National Aptitude Test in Architecture (NATA) conducted by Council of Architecture COA), and as per score in JEE-(mains) Paper-II.

Bachelor of Technology in Digital Techniques in Design and Planning (DTDP), Facilities and Services Planning (FSP) and Planning - AP-EAMCET conducted by Government of Andhra Pradesh.

Bachelor of Fine Arts and Bachelor of Design - Art and Design Common Entrance Test (AD- CET) conducted by Dr. YSR Architecture and Fine Arts University, Kadapa.

Seats will be allotted as per the merit list in the qualifying examination and as per the government norms for reservation candidates.

Admissions will be based on the score obtained in the written Entrance Examination followed by verification of qualifying certificates. The final merit list will be prepared on the basis of the final Score/Written Test marks (as applicable).


For Fine Arts and Interior Design course, AD-CET results will be declared on APSCHE website after which students can apply for online admission's counselling. Short listed candidates will be sent Admit card and they will need to get Certificates verified and pay the stipulated fee to confirm the admission in the University. Depending on vacancy a second merit list will be prepared and remaining seats will be filled accordingly.


For B-Tech courses, AP-EAMCET results will be declared on the State Council of Higher Education website. The students have to attend for online admission conducted by convener AP-EAMCET and select the preferred course. Short listed candidates will be sent Admit card and they will need to get Certificates verified and pay the stipulated fee to confirm the admission in the University. Depending on vacancy a second merit list will be prepared and remaining seats will be filled accordingly.


For B.Arch. courses, NATA results will be declared on Council of Architecture's website. The students have to attend for online admission by Convener APSCHE and select the preferred course. (Students who wrote as per score in JEE(main) paper-II.) Short listed candidates will be sent Admit card and they will need to get Certificates verified and pay the stipulated fee to confirm the admission in the University. Depending on vacancy a second merit list will be prepared and remaining seats will be filled accordingly.
